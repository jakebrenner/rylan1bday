Frequently Asked Questions

Pricing FAQ

Everything you need to know about Ryvite pricing. Have more questions? Visit our full FAQ page.

How much does Ryvite cost?
Ryvite is free to start with pay-as-you-go pricing. You only pay for what you use -- there are no subscriptions, no monthly fees, and no per-card charges. Every event includes unlimited guests, an AI-generated custom design, RSVP tracking, and unique guest links. You can design your invitation for free and only pay when you are ready to publish.
Are there per-card or per-guest fees?
No. Unlike Paperless Post (which charges $1-$8 per card) and Greenvelope (which charges $0.50-$3 per card), Ryvite never charges per card or per guest. With pay-as-you-go pricing, you can invite as many guests as you want -- 10 or 1,000 -- with no per-card fees.
Is there a subscription or monthly fee?
No subscriptions. Ryvite uses simple pay-as-you-go pricing. You only pay for what you use. There are no recurring charges, no annual plans, and no hidden fees.
What is included in the price?
Every Ryvite event includes: a custom AI-generated invitation design (not a template), RSVP tracking and guest management, unique shareable links per guest, a mobile-optimized experience, a completely ad-free invitation page, and unlimited design refinements until you love it. There are no feature tiers or premium upgrades.
How does Ryvite pricing compare to Paperless Post?
Paperless Post charges between $1 and $8 per card, which means inviting 100 guests could cost anywhere from $100 to $800. Ryvite uses pay-as-you-go pricing with unlimited guests -- you only pay for what you use, no matter how many people you invite. Ryvite also uses AI to create truly custom designs rather than selecting from a library of templates. See the full comparison table above.
Can I try Ryvite before paying?
Yes. You can create your invitation completely free. You describe your event, our AI generates a custom invitation design, and you can refine it as many times as you like. You only pay when you are satisfied with the result and ready to publish your event and send invitations to your guest list.
