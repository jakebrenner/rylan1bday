Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating AI-generated invitations, tracking RSVPs, pricing, and more.

Getting Started

How does Ryvite work?
Ryvite uses AI to create custom digital invitations in under 30 seconds. You describe your event in plain language, our AI generates a unique invitation design, you share a link with your guests, and you track RSVPs from your dashboard in real time. The entire process from description to shareable invite takes less than a minute.
Do I need design skills to use Ryvite?
No design skills are needed. Just describe your event in plain language — mention the vibe, colors, or mood you want, and the AI handles all the design work. You can say something like "elegant garden party with soft pastels" or "bold neon 80s birthday bash" and the AI translates that into a fully designed invitation.
What event types does Ryvite support?
Ryvite supports every event type including birthdays, baby showers, weddings, graduations, holiday parties, corporate events, dinner parties, anniversaries, and more. If you can describe it, the AI can design an invitation for it.
How fast is AI invitation generation?
Your initial invitation is generated in under 30 seconds. If you want to make changes afterward, refinements through the design chat take just 5 to 15 seconds each. Most users have a finished, shareable invitation in under two minutes from start to finish.
Can I see examples before creating my own?
Yes. Visit the inspiration gallery to browse AI-generated invitation designs across different event types and styles. Every example was created using the same AI that will generate your invitation, so what you see is representative of what you'll get.

Pricing & Payment

How much does Ryvite cost?
Ryvite is free to start with pay-as-you-go pricing. There are no subscriptions, no per-guest fees, and no hidden charges. You only pay for what you use, regardless of how many guests you invite. Visit the pricing page for current details.
Are there per-invite or per-guest fees?
No. Ryvite uses pay-as-you-go pricing with unlimited guests. Send your invitation to 10 people or 500 — there are no per-card, per-invite, or per-guest fees, which makes Ryvite especially cost-effective for larger events.
How does Ryvite's pricing compare to Paperless Post?
Paperless Post charges $1 to $8 per card, which means inviting 100 guests can cost $100 to $800. Ryvite is free to start with pay-as-you-go pricing and unlimited guests, making it dramatically more affordable. For a 100-guest event, you could save hundreds of dollars compared to per-card pricing models.
Can I try Ryvite before paying?
Yes. You can create and preview your AI-generated invitation before committing to a purchase. See exactly what your invitation will look like, make refinements through the design chat, and only pay when you're ready to publish and share with your guests.

Design & Customization

What does "AI-generated" mean for invitations?
Unlike template-based platforms where you pick from pre-made designs, every Ryvite invitation is uniquely created by AI based on your description. The typography, colors, layout, and decorative illustrations are all generated fresh for your specific event. You're not choosing from a catalog — you're getting a one-of-a-kind design.
Can I customize my invitation after it's generated?
Yes. After the initial generation, you can use the design chat to request changes in plain language. Say things like "make the background darker," "change the font to something more playful," or "swap the date to Saturday." Changes are applied in seconds, and you can iterate as many times as you need.
Can I upload inspiration photos to guide the design?
Yes. You can upload photos during the creation process to guide the AI's design choices. The AI analyzes your image for color palette, mood, and style, then incorporates those elements into your invitation. This works great with venue photos, Pinterest inspiration, or even a color swatch.
Are Ryvite designs really unique?
Yes. Every invitation is generated fresh from your description — there is no template library. Even if two people describe similar events, the AI produces different designs each time. Your invitation is genuinely one of a kind, not a slightly modified version of something thousands of others have used.

RSVP & Guest Management

Can I track RSVPs with Ryvite?
Yes. Ryvite includes built-in RSVP tracking with every invitation. Guests respond directly on the invite page, and you can monitor responses in real time from your admin dashboard. You can also add custom RSVP fields like meal preferences, plus-ones, dietary restrictions, or any other information you need to collect.
What do guests see when they receive my invitation?
Guests see a beautiful, full-screen invitation that is fully mobile-optimized. There are no ads, no platform branding cluttering the design, and no account required to RSVP. The experience is clean, fast, and focused entirely on your event — exactly as an invitation should be.
Can multiple people manage an event?
Yes. Ryvite supports a co-host feature that lets multiple people manage the same event. Co-hosts can view RSVPs, track guest responses, and help manage event details from their own dashboards. This is especially useful for weddings, corporate events, or any celebration with shared planning responsibilities.

Privacy & Technical

Is my data private on Ryvite?
Yes. Ryvite does not sell your data, does not display ads on your invitations, and does not share your information with third parties. Your event details and guest information are kept private and secure. We believe your invitation should celebrate your event, not monetize your data.
Is Ryvite mobile-friendly?
Yes. Ryvite is built with a mobile-first approach. Both the invitation creation process and the guest-facing invitation pages are fully optimized for phones, tablets, and desktops. Your guests will have a seamless experience on any device, with no pinching, zooming, or awkward layouts.
