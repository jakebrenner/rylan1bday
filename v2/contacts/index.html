Enable Contact Picker on Safari

Safari requires a one-time setting change to access your phone contacts:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Scroll down and tap Safari
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Advanced
  4. Tap Feature Flags
  5. Find Contact Picker API and toggle it ON
  6. Come back here and tap From Phone again
