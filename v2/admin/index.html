How to set up tracking in Facebook Ads Manager
  1. Upload the downloaded video as your ad creative
  2. In ad setup, find "URL parameters" under Tracking
  3. Paste the URL parameters copied above
  4. Set the Website URL to your destination without UTM params
  5. Facebook will append the parameters automatically to every click

This ensures every click is attributed back to the exact creative for reporting.
